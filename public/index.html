A Little About My Journey

Hello! I’m Kehinde Muraina, a passionate management expert committed to driving change and innovation across Africa and beyond. With over 7 years of diverse experience, I leverage technology and design to create sustainable solutions that foster development.

As a TEDx Speaker and Tony Elumelu mentor, I share my insights with aspiring leaders and contribute to transformative initiatives. When I'm not focused on my professional endeavors, I enjoy exploring new ideas, speaking at events, and mentoring emerging leaders. I believe in the power of collaboration and look forward to connecting with others to create positive change for a brighter future.
